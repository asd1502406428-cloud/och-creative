一、我們可能收集的資料1. Data We May Collect

當您使用 YouCan 自律盲盒時，我們可能收集下列資料類型：When you use YouCan Mystery Box, we may collect the following types of data:

  • 使用者識別資料：例如匿名使用者 ID，用於識別同一安裝實例、維持功能狀態、同步資料與提供應用程式功能。User identification data: such as an anonymous user ID, used to identify the same app installation, maintain feature status, synchronize data, and provide app functionality.
  • 名稱或暱稱：若您主動於應用程式中輸入名稱、暱稱或類似資訊，我們可能會儲存該資料。Name or nickname: if you voluntarily enter a name, nickname, or similar information in the app, we may store that information.
  • 應用程式活動與互動資料：例如開啟應用程式、抽取盲盒、完成任務、領取獎勵、點擊功能、頁面互動與其他使用行為事件。App activity and interaction data: such as app opens, mystery box draws, task completion, reward claims, feature clicks, page interactions, and other usage events.
  • 使用者提供內容：例如您建立的自訂挑戰、任務名稱、任務描述或其他由您主動輸入的內容。User-provided content: such as custom challenges, task names, task descriptions, or other content you voluntarily enter.
  • 統計與分析資料：我們可能根據前述資料建立聚合分析、產品優化與服務改善所需的衍生資料。Statistics and analytics data: we may create aggregated or derived data based on the above information for product optimization and service improvement.

二、我們如何使用資料2. How We Use Data

我們可能基於以下目的使用資料：We may use data for the following purposes:

  • 提供、維護與改善本應用程式的核心功能；to provide, maintain, and improve the core features of the app;
  • 保存任務進度、成長紀錄、獎勵狀態與互動結果；to save task progress, growth records, reward status, and interaction results;
  • 分析使用情況，以優化功能設計、穩定性與整體使用體驗；to analyze usage in order to improve feature design, stability, and overall user experience;
  • 避免異常操作、重複領取或其他影響系統正常運作的情形；to prevent abnormal operations, duplicate claims, or other issues that may affect normal system operation;
  • 在必要情況下履行安全性、服務維護或法規遵循需求。to meet security, service maintenance, or legal compliance needs when necessary.
使用原則Usage Principle 我們僅會依據目前版本中已實際提供的功能處理資料，不會因未來可能推出但尚未上線的功能，而額外收集不必要資訊。 We only process data based on features that are actually available in the current version. We do not collect unnecessary information for future features that have not yet been released.

三、資料分享與第三方服務3. Data Sharing and Third-party Services

我們不會出售您的個人資料。為提供本應用程式之服務，我們可能使用第三方雲端基礎設施、資料庫或後端技術服務，以協助資料處理與儲存。We do not sell your personal data. To provide the app service, we may use third-party cloud infrastructure, databases, or backend technology services to support data processing and storage.

這些服務提供者僅於提供服務所必要的範圍內處理資料，並不代表我們將您的資料出售、出租或用於與本應用程式無關之用途。These service providers process data only within the scope necessary to provide their services. This does not mean that we sell, rent, or use your data for purposes unrelated to this app.

四、資料傳輸與安全4. Data Transfer and Security

我們採取合理之技術與管理措施保護資料安全，並在適用情況下透過加密連線傳輸資料。然而，任何網際網路傳輸或電子儲存方式，均無法保證絕對安全。We use reasonable technical and administrative measures to protect data security and, where applicable, transmit data through encrypted connections. However, no method of internet transmission or electronic storage can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

五、資料保存與刪除5. Data Retention and Deletion

我們會在提供功能、分析服務品質、維護系統穩定性及履行法律義務所需之期間內保存資料。We retain data for as long as necessary to provide features, analyze service quality, maintain system stability, and comply with legal obligations.

若您希望刪除與您相關的資料，可透過電子郵件與我們聯絡。由於本應用程式目前可能採匿名識別方式運作，建議您於來信時提供可協助辨識之資訊，例如名稱、相關使用情境、畫面截圖或其他可供定位資料之說明。If you would like to delete data related to you, you may contact us by email. Because the app may currently operate through anonymous identifiers, we recommend including information that helps us locate the relevant data, such as your name, usage context, screenshots, or other identifying details.

六、權限使用說明6. Permission Use

本應用程式僅會於功能需要時請求必要權限。若特定版本使用裝置權限，該權限將僅用於對應功能，不會超出該功能目的進行不必要之資料蒐集。The app only requests permissions when they are necessary for a feature. If a specific version uses device permissions, those permissions are used only for their corresponding features and not for unnecessary data collection beyond that purpose.

若您目前版本仍包含麥克風權限，其用途僅限於應用程式對應功能，不會在未經您知情與授權之情況下進行錄音、儲存或上傳音訊內容。If your current version still includes microphone permission, it is used only for the corresponding app feature. The app will not record, store, or upload audio content without your knowledge and authorization.
