主打作品：YouCan 自律盲盒 The Main Work: YouCan Mystery Box

這是 OCH Creative 的第一款核心產品，也是我們對「自律」這件事的回答。 下面四個步驟，是它從打開到留下來的完整邏輯。 YouCan is our first core product — our answer to what "self-discipline" should actually feel like. Below are the four mechanics that make it work, from first tap to lasting habit.

01 / 每日任務 01 / Daily Task

不規劃，先打開 Just open, don't plan

不用先設計一整套生活系統。每天從一個小任務開始，先動起來，剩下的讓累積去說話。 No need to design a whole life system upfront. Start each day with one small task. Get moving first — let accumulation do the rest.

02 / 陪伴 02 / Companion

不是打勾，是養成 Not checking off — raising

完成任務不只在清單上劃線。寵物會跟著你成長、心情會回應你的節奏，讓自律有了體溫。 Finishing a task is more than crossing a line. The pet grows alongside you, its mood follows your rhythm — discipline gets a heartbeat.

03 / 獎勵系統 03 / Reward System

看得見的累積 Progress you can see

徽章牆、碎片與商城把抽象的努力變成可視化的成就。明天還會想回來，因為昨天的努力沒有消失。 A badge wall, collectible fragments, and an in-app shop turn invisible effort into something you can see. You come back tomorrow because yesterday didn't vanish.

04 / 輕量自律 04 / Lightweight Discipline

不逼你完美 Not built to push you

YouCan 不想做一個逼人完美的工具。它在意的是：今天有沒有比昨天，多走一點點。 YouCan isn't designed to push you toward perfection. It only cares whether today moved a little further than yesterday.

和一般打卡 APP，到底差在哪？ So what makes it different from a habit tracker?

兩種思路，兩種使用者體感。同樣是「養成」，差別在於：你是被工具監督，還是被產品陪著走。 Two philosophies, two completely different experiences. Same goal of "habit building" — but one supervises you, the other walks with you.

普通打卡工具 Ordinary Trackers

  • 只記錄完成與否，回饋停留在「是/否」。 Records completion as a binary — done or not.
  • 很容易演變成壓力清單，用久就累。 Easily turns into a pressure list. Burns you out over time.
  • 缺少每天打開它的理由，只剩責任。 No real reason to come back — only obligation.

YouCan 自律盲盒 YouCan Mystery Box

  • 用盲盒機制降低「開始」的心理門檻。 Mystery-box mechanics lower the cost of starting.
  • 寵物、徽章、等級、碎片——多層回饋取代單一勾選。 Pets, badges, levels, fragments — layered feedback instead of one checkbox.
  • 把自律變成你願意明天再回來的事。 Turns discipline into something you actually want to come back to.
