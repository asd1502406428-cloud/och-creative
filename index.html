01 / 每日任務 01 / Daily Task

每日任務盲盒 Daily Task Box

使用者不需要一開始就規劃一堆宏大目標。每天打開一個小任務，先開始，再慢慢累積。 Users do not need to plan a huge system from day one. They open one small task each day, start first, and build momentum over time.

02 / 陪伴 02 / Companion

寵物陪伴成長 Companion Pet Growth

完成任務不只是打勾，而是讓寵物、等級與情緒狀態一起變化，把自律轉成更有生命感的回饋。 Completing a task is not just checking a box. Pets, levels, and mood feedback make self-discipline feel more alive.

03 / 獎勵系統 03 / Reward System

徽章、等級與碎片獎勵 Badges, Levels, and Fragments

用可視化成就記錄持續努力。徽章牆、碎片與商城讓使用者有下一次打開 APP 的理由。 Visual achievements record consistent effort. Badges, fragments, and the shop give users a reason to come back.

04 / 輕量自律 04 / Lightweight Discipline

不是高壓打卡，而是低壓持續 Less Pressure, More Continuity

YouCan 的核心不是逼迫使用者完美，而是降低開始門檻，讓每天完成一點點變得更容易。 YouCan is not built to pressure users into perfection. It lowers the starting barrier and makes small daily progress easier.

為什麼不是普通打卡 APP Why It Is Not Just Another Habit Tracker

這一段用來補足產品說服力，讓新使用者快速理解 YouCan 與普通待辦、打卡工具的差異。 This section strengthens product persuasion. New users need to understand how YouCan differs from ordinary to-do or habit tools.

普通打卡工具 Ordinary Trackers

  • 主要記錄完成與否，回饋比較單一。 Mainly records completion, with limited feedback.
  • 容易變成壓力清單，用久後疲勞。 Often becomes a pressure list and causes fatigue over time.
  • 缺少每日期待感，使用理由不夠強。 Lacks daily anticipation and a strong reason to return.

YouCan 自律盲盒 YouCan Mystery Box

  • 用盲盒任務降低開始門檻。 Uses mystery-box tasks to lower the starting barrier.
  • 用寵物、徽章、等級與碎片提供多層回饋。 Provides layered feedback through pets, badges, levels, and fragments.
  • 把自律包裝成更輕、更願意持續的日常體驗。 Turns discipline into a lighter and more sustainable daily experience.
